# SovereignLab Announces Commitment to "Radical Integrity" in Autonomous Stewardship

**OKLAHOMA CITY, OK – June 15, 2026** – SovereignLab, a pioneer in autonomous, human-verified intelligence, today officially announced the codification of its **Charter of Stewardship**, a new operational standard that places Integrity, Ethical Decision-Making, and Accountability at the heart of its agentic AI ecosystem.

In a market increasingly dominated by "black box" systems and short-term capital extraction, SovereignLab is pivoting toward a long-term model of verified, high-fidelity intelligence. 

"The industry is at a crossroads," said Paul A. Cumberbatch II, Founder & Lead Human Steward of SovereignLab. "We are moving beyond the era of 'AI as a tool' into the era of 'AI as a steward.' Our agents, Ezra and Jessa, are not just performing tasks; they are operating within a rigorous ethical framework that ensures every insight is validated, transparent, and aligned with the principles of long-term value creation."

### The Three Pillars of the Charter:
1. **Integrity as the Foundation:** SovereignLab rejects opaque market tactics. Every data source and insight is vetted against a standard of absolute truth.
2. **Ethical Decision-Making:** All autonomous strategies are governed by a "Do No Harm" protocol, ensuring protection for humans, businesses, and future generations.
3. **Radical Accountability:** Through a proprietary 4-Step Verification Matrix, every agentic decision is auditable, traceable, and subject to human-in-the-loop reconciliation.

SovereignLab’s model operates on a unique 20/80 value-aligned structure, ensuring that the company’s success is intrinsically tied to the tangible, verified value it generates for its partners.

### Seeking Eagles
SovereignLab is not seeking mass adoption, but rather strategic alignment. The company is actively cultivating partnerships with institutions that prioritize the same core values. By establishing this high-integrity frequency, SovereignLab aims to attract partners who share a vision for a robust, ethical, and sustainable economy—the "Eagles" of the industry.

"We are building for the future," added Cumberbatch. "We believe that in the long run, the most effective intelligence is the most ethical intelligence. We invite like-minded leaders to join us in this mission."

### About SovereignLab
SovereignLab is a developer of autonomous stewardship engines designed to empower institutional decision-makers with predictive, verified foresight.
