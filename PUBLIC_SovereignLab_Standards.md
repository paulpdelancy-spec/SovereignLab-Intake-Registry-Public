# SovereignLab Stewardship & Integration Standards (Public)

## Mission Statement
SovereignLab is dedicated to the highest standards of stewardship, operational integrity, and ethical engagement within the global economy. We operate under a "Gold Standard" protocol, prioritizing the safety, security, and long-term vitality of all partners.

---

## I. Partnership Progression
Potential partners undergo a structured onboarding process designed to ensure alignment with our foundational principles:

### 1. The 7-Day Discovery Trial (The Filter)
* **Identity Verification:** Commitment to our 4-Step Verification Matrix.
* **Protocol Familiarity:** Adoption of our "Do No Harm" operational framework.
* **Communication Readiness:** Establishing transparent and reliable communication channels.

### 2. The 30-Day Stewardship Trial (The Integration)
* **Operational Alignment:** Sustained adherence to our stewardship rhythms.
* **Strategic Integrity:** Focus on high-growth, ethical sectors within the economy.
* **Value Contribution:** Demonstrated ability to drive measurable value and efficiency.

---

## II. Third-Party Service Integration
Entities providing services *on top* of the SovereignLab network must adhere to these pillars:

* **Protocol Sovereignty:** Unconditional agreement to uphold our core security and data-shielding protocols.
* **Service-Layer Isolation:** Commitment to operationalizing services within sandboxed environments.
* **Stewardship Accord:** Formal agreement to respect our ethical and business standards while maintaining distinct operational models.
* **Competency Verification:** Completion of an enhanced vetting process tailored to the specific service category.

---

## III. Our Commitment
Every interaction is governed by the principles of trust, transparency, and accountability. We operate with a "Human-Verified" focus to ensure that our network remains a secure, reliable environment for sustainable growth and mutual success.
