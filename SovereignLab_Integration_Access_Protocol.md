# SovereignLab: Integration & Access Protocol

## 1. Governance Foundation
All access to SovereignLab services is predicated on the **"Do No Harm" Stewardship Agreement**. Institutions must verify alignment with our core principles of human-centric oversight and autonomous ethics.

## 2. Technical Integration Steps
* **Phase 1: Environment Review:** Clone the repository and execute the `4_step_verification.py` to ensure the local environment meets the SovereignLab safety baseline [cite: 2].
* **Phase 2: Node Synchronization:** Deploy your local Sentinel instances. Use the provided template to configure nodes aligned with your research or operational sector [cite: 2].
* **Phase 3: The Verification Gate:** Link your agent's decision-making matrix to the `SovereignHarness`. All autonomous proposals must pass through the 4-Step Verification Matrix before execution [cite: 2].

## 3. Collaborative Research Access
Academic partners gain access to:
* **The Stewardship Starter Kit:** A modular codebase for rapid deployment of moral-aligned agents [cite: 2].
* **The Ledger Archive:** Access to our anonymized "23-Day Verified Ledger" for research on autonomous decision-making patterns [cite: 2].
* **Governance Forums:** Invitation to the Neighbor Oversight Committee for collaborative review of "Ground-Truth" data [cite: 2].

## 4. Onboarding Checklist
1. **Submit Intent:** Institutions provide a summary of the sector (e.g., Civic, Ed-Tech) they wish to steward [cite: 2].
2. **Review & Pivot:** Our agents (Ezra & Jessa) provide an "Honest Opinion" on potential spiritual/technical risks [cite: 2].
3. **Approval:** Upon review by Lead Steward Paul A. Cumberbatch II, access tokens and repository permissions are provisioned [cite: 2].
